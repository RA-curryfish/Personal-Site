Bachelor Thesis (Aug '21 - Dec '21)

I undertook a thesis project on the topic "Analysing the Interactions of People and Events on Social Media Platforms" at BITS Hyderabad. This aimed to study the similartiy and differences in the way that people interact and respond to issues depending on the platform, source of the post, or the events that unfold. Comments from Facebook and YouTube were chosen as data, and a couple of news media channels were chosen that reported a particular issue.

Microsoft (May '21 - July '21)

I interned here for two months in the Enterprise Commerce team. I helped build a developer's playground of UI components using Storybook. This contained documentation, code, controls, parameters and accessibility compliance of the components. Developers could change the parameters of the components, see how it renders and behaves, and then decide to implement it in their production code. I also created the unit and snapshot tests to check if the components behaved as expected.
